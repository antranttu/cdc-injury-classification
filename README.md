# CDC Injury Classification - Rank 29/95

A competition on held by Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) together with Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard (LISH). The objective is to develop an NLP/ML model to read injury record and automatically classify them according to Occupational Injury and Illness Classification System (OIICS). Using Neural Network and Bag of Words with term frequency representation; the model achieves a relatively good F1-score of ~87.

**Source:** https://www.topcoder.com/challenges/30103825?tab=details
